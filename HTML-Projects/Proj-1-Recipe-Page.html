Chocolate Chip Cookies

This classic recipe makes delicious, chewy chocolate chip cookies perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Mix in flour and stir until combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips and scoop onto baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
